About Me

Hi! I'm Ollie. I'm currently a PhD student at COMPASS CDT at the Univeristy of Bristol. Before that, I studied an MSci in Computer Science and Maths at the University of Exeter.

At the moment, my research focuses on the entropy of soft random geometric graphs (hence the background to this page!).

This is my personal website, where you'll find information about me, my current research, and more generally, things I find interesting.
