About Me

I'm currently a first-year PhD student at the University of Bristol, as a part of COMPASS CDT. My research interests are in random geometric graphs, particularly in the context of wireless networks. I'm currently working on the problem of quantifying the entropy of soft random geometric graphs, which are random geometric graphs with probabilistic connection functions. I explain this in more detail in my research page.
